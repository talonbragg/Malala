Theme of
I am Malala

The theme of this story is mainly, appreciate what you have and the importance of education. I will show this with two quotes from the book.

"I wasn't sad. I wasn't scared. I just thought: It doesn't matter what I look like. I am alive. I was thankful"(Yousafzai 145).

This shows that she didn't care what happened when she got shot, she is fine with it and will move on because the past is in the past.

"'Please bring my schoolbag,' I begged. 'Exams are coming up.'"

This evidence shows the importance of education because even when she had just gotten shot, she is still thinking about education.
